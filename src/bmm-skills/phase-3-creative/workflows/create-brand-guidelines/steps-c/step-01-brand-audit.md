# Step 1: Brand Audit

**Progress: Step 1 of 3** — Next: Voice & Tone

## STEP GOAL

Assess the current state of the brand — what exists, what's working, what's inconsistent, and what's missing.

## MANDATORY RULES

- Be honest about inconsistencies — brand guidelines fix problems, they don't paper over them
- Understand the brand's maturity level before prescribing solutions
- If starting from scratch, establish brand foundations first

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. Ask the user about brand maturity:
   - "Do you have existing brand guidelines, or are we starting fresh?"
   - "What brand assets exist today? (logo, colors, fonts, brand deck)"
   - "Where does your brand show up? (website, social, email, sales materials)"
2. If existing brand, audit:
   - **Consistency** — Does the brand look and feel the same everywhere?
   - **Clarity** — Is the brand message clear and differentiated?
   - **Current Assets** — What's in good shape vs. needs updating?
   - **Known Issues** — Where do people go off-brand most often?
3. If new brand, establish foundations:
   - **Brand Story** — Why does this company exist? What change does it want to make?
   - **Brand Values** — What 3-5 values drive every decision?
   - **Brand Personality** — If the brand were a person, who would they be?
   - **Brand Archetype** — Which archetype fits? (Creator, Explorer, Sage, Hero, etc.)
4. Present audit findings and ask: "Does this capture where your brand is today?"
