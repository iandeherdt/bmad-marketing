# Step 2: Channel Setup

**Progress: Step 2 of 4** — Next: Content Deployment

## STEP GOAL

Configure each marketing channel with the right targeting, budgets, UTM conventions, and technical settings.

## MANDATORY RULES

- Every link must have UTM parameters
- Budget must be set with daily/total caps
- Targeting must match the audience personas from research/strategy

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. For each channel in the campaign, create a setup guide:

   **Paid Ads (if applicable)**
   - Platform and account
   - Campaign structure (campaigns → ad groups → ads)
   - Targeting: audience, demographics, interests, lookalikes
   - Budget: daily cap and total budget
   - Bid strategy
   - Ad formats and placements
   - Schedule (start/end, dayparting)

   **Email (if applicable)**
   - Email platform / tool
   - List or segment to target
   - Sequence structure (how many emails, timing)
   - From name and reply-to
   - Subject line A/B test setup

   **Social (if applicable)**
   - Platforms and accounts
   - Post schedule (dates and times)
   - Hashtag strategy
   - Engagement plan (who responds to comments?)

   **Content / SEO (if applicable)**
   - Publication schedule
   - SEO keywords targeted
   - Internal linking plan
   - Distribution plan (newsletter, social, syndication)

2. Define **UTM Convention**:
   - `utm_source`: {{platform}}
   - `utm_medium`: {{paid/organic/email}}
   - `utm_campaign`: {{campaign_name}}
   - `utm_content`: {{creative_variant}}
   - `utm_term`: {{keyword if applicable}}

3. Present setup plan and ask: "Anything I'm missing for your channels?"
