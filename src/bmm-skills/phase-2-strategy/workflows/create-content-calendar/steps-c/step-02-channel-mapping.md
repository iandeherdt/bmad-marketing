# Step 2: Channel Mapping

**Progress: Step 2 of 3** — Next: Calendar Build

## STEP GOAL

Map content types and formats to channels, defining what kind of content goes where and how often.

## MANDATORY RULES

- Channel selection must match audience habits and available resources
- Each channel needs a clear content format and cadence
- Don't overcommit — a realistic cadence beats an ambitious one you can't sustain

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. If channel strategy exists, pull from it. Otherwise, identify active channels:
   - Blog / Website
   - LinkedIn
   - Twitter / X
   - Instagram
   - Email newsletter
   - YouTube / Video
   - Podcast
   - Other
2. For each active channel, define:
   - **Content Format** — What type of content? (article, short post, carousel, video, etc.)
   - **Pillar Mix** — Which pillars does this channel cover?
   - **Cadence** — How often? (daily, 3x/week, weekly, biweekly, monthly)
   - **Best Practices** — Optimal length, timing, hashtags, etc.
   - **Repurpose From** — Can content from one channel feed another?
3. Build a content-channel matrix:

   | Channel | Format | Cadence | Primary Pillars | Repurposed From |
   |---------|--------|---------|----------------|-----------------|

4. Present and ask: "Is this cadence sustainable with your resources?"
