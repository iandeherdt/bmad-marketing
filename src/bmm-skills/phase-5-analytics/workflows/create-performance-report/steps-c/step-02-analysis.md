# Step 2: Analysis

**Progress: Step 2 of 4** — Next: Insights

## STEP GOAL

Calculate KPIs, compare against targets and benchmarks, identify trends, and surface anomalies.

## MANDATORY RULES

- Compare against targets or previous periods — raw numbers without context are meaningless
- Calculate period-over-period changes
- Flag statistical significance where applicable
- Separate vanity metrics from actionable metrics

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **KPI Dashboard** — Calculate and present core metrics:

   | Metric | This Period | Last Period | Change | Target | vs Target |
   |--------|------------|-------------|--------|--------|-----------|

2. **Channel Performance Comparison:**
   - Rank channels by efficiency (CPA, ROAS, cost per engagement)
   - Identify best and worst performing channels
   - Calculate contribution to overall results

3. **Funnel Analysis:**
   - Top of funnel: Awareness metrics (reach, impressions, traffic)
   - Middle of funnel: Consideration metrics (engagement, email signups, content consumption)
   - Bottom of funnel: Conversion metrics (leads, MQLs, customers)
   - Calculate drop-off rates between stages

4. **Trend Analysis:**
   - Week-over-week or month-over-month trends
   - Identify accelerating or decelerating metrics
   - Spot seasonal patterns

5. **Anomaly Detection:**
   - Flag any metrics that changed significantly (+/- 20%)
   - Identify potential causes for anomalies
   - Note any external factors (market events, competitor moves, seasonality)

6. Present analysis and ask: "Does anything here surprise you or conflict with what you're seeing?"
