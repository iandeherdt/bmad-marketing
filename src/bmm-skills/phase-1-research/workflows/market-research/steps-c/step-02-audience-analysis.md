# Step 2: Audience Analysis

**Progress: Step 2 of 4** — Next: Competitive Intel

## STEP GOAL

Build detailed audience personas with demographics, psychographics, pain points, buying triggers, and channel preferences.

## MANDATORY RULES

- Build on the scope defined in Step 1
- Always validate personas with the user — they know their customers
- Create at least 1 primary and 1 secondary persona
- Include both rational (needs) and emotional (motivations) dimensions

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. Ask the user: "Tell me about your ideal customer. Who are they? What's their day-to-day like?"
2. Probe deeper:
   - "What problem keeps them up at night that you solve?"
   - "How do they currently solve this problem without you?"
   - "Where do they spend time online? What do they read/watch/listen to?"
   - "Who influences their decisions?"
   - "What would make them say 'shut up and take my money'?"
3. Build each persona with:
   - **Name & Role** (fictional but representative)
   - **Demographics** (age, role, company size, industry)
   - **Goals & Motivations** (what they're trying to achieve)
   - **Pain Points** (what's blocking them)
   - **Buying Triggers** (what makes them take action)
   - **Channel Preferences** (where to reach them)
   - **Objections** (why they might say no)
4. Present personas and ask for validation
