# Step 4: Research Synthesis

**Progress: Step 4 of 4** — Final Step

## STEP GOAL

Synthesize all research findings into an actionable market research brief with clear recommendations for the strategy phase.

## MANDATORY RULES

- Reference specific findings from Steps 1-3
- Prioritize insights by impact on marketing decisions
- Every insight should connect to a recommendation
- Use the market research brief template for the final output

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. Review all work from Steps 1-3:
   - Research scope and questions from Step 1
   - Audience personas from Step 2
   - Competitive landscape from Step 3
2. Synthesize into the research brief using `templates/market-research-brief.md`:
   - **Executive Summary** — 3-5 sentence overview of the market opportunity
   - **Key Findings** — Top 5-7 insights, ranked by strategic importance
   - **Audience Summary** — Personas with priority ranking
   - **Competitive Position** — Where you stand and where you can win
   - **Opportunities** — Top 3-5 strategic opportunities
   - **Risks & Threats** — What could go wrong
   - **Recommendations** — Specific actions for the strategy phase
3. Present the synthesized brief to the user
4. Ask: "Does this capture the market picture accurately? Anything to add or adjust?"
5. Save to `docs/output/market-research-brief.md`
6. Save competitive landscape to `docs/output/competitive-landscape.md`
