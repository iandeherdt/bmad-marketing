## Executive Summary

R.T.A. Construct has an open window to become the go-to construction partner for property developers and investors in Limburg, Antwerp, and Flemish Brabant. No competitor owns this positioning, and favorable market conditions (EPC renovation obligations, 6% VAT expansion) are driving new development activity. This strategy focuses on repositioning R.T.A.'s proven "no surprises" model for a B2B audience, using June/July 2026 project completions as a proof-point launch moment, with the goal of doubling the sales pipeline by year-end.

## Channel Strategy

### Channel Mix

| Channel | Role | Content Type | Cadence | Resource % | Primary KPI |
|---------|------|-------------|---------|------------|-------------|
| LinkedIn | Awareness + Consideration | Project milestones, regulatory insights, team spotlights | 2-3x/week | 35% | Follower growth, engagement, inbound connections |
| Website + SEO | Consideration + Conversion | B2B section, case studies, blog posts | 2 posts/month + case studies | 30% | Organic B2B traffic, quote requests |
| Embuild + Events | Awareness + Relationships | Networking, case study distribution | 2-3 events/year | 20% | Qualified contacts, follow-up meetings |
| Email Newsletter | Nurture + Retention | Project updates, market insights | Monthly | 10% | Open rate, click rate, meetings booked |
| Instagram/Facebook | Maintenance | Repurposed project photos | Occasional | 5% | N/A (maintain, don't invest) |

### Primary Channels

**1. LinkedIn** — First mover opportunity for B2B construction content in the region. Company page + leadership personal profiles. Content: project completions (photos/video), regulatory insights (EPC, VAT), team spotlights, partnership stories. Consider LinkedIn Ads in Q3 if organic gains traction.

**2. Website + SEO** — The validation layer. When developers hear about R.T.A. through word of mouth, they'll search online. Website must convert the "verification visit" with a dedicated developer/investor section, case studies with project data, and targeted SEO content. Key search terms: "aannemer grootschalige projecten Limburg", "bouwpartner projectontwikkelaars", "sleutel-op-de-deur appartementen".

**3. Embuild + Trade Events** — High-trust, face-to-face channel. Target events: Realty (Sep 2026), Futurebuild Belgium (Jan 2027), Embuild Limburg & Antwerp regional events. Attend first, sponsor later.

### Secondary Channel

**4. Email Newsletter** — Monthly touchpoint to stay top-of-mind with contacts from LinkedIn, events, and website. Low effort, high compounding value over time.

## Campaign Plan

### Phase 1: "Build the Foundation" (April – May 2026)
- **Objective:** Establish B2B presence and infrastructure before the June/July proof moment
- **Audience:** Both personas (Marc the developer + Sofie the investor)
- **Key Message:** "Your construction partner, not just your contractor"
- **Channels:** Website, LinkedIn
- **Timeline:** April 1 – May 31
- **Tactics:**
  1. Build dedicated "For Developers & Investors" website section
  2. Launch LinkedIn company page strategy + activate leadership personal profiles
  3. Publish 2 SEO blog posts (EPC renovation obligations, 6% VAT opportunities)
  4. Join Embuild Limburg + Embuild Antwerpen
  5. Set up email tool and newsletter template
  6. Begin documenting June/July projects for case studies (behind-the-scenes photos, timeline tracking, budget data)
- **Budget:** Website development (one-time), Embuild memberships, email tool subscription
- **KPIs:** Website B2B section live by Apr 30; LinkedIn posting 2x/week by mid-April; 100+ LinkedIn followers by end of May; 2 blog posts published; Embuild memberships active

### Phase 2: "Prove It" (June – August 2026)
- **Objective:** Use project completions to generate social proof and first qualified leads
- **Audience:** Primary: Marc (developers), Secondary: Sofie (investors)
- **Key Message:** "Fixed price. Fixed team. Fixed timeline. Here's the proof."
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, Website, Email, Events
- **Timeline:** June 1 – August 31
- **Tactics:**
  1. Professional photography + video of completed June/July projects
  2. Publish 2 detailed case studies (scope, timeline, budget adherence, client testimonial)
  3. Host 1-2 invite-only site visits for targeted developers/investors (10-15 people per event)
  4. LinkedIn campaign: project completion story series (3 posts/week)
  5. Launch monthly newsletter — first edition features case study + market insight
  6. Publish 2 more SEO blog posts targeting developer search terms
  7. Prepare for Realty (Sep 2026): identify target contacts, print case study materials
- **Budget:** Professional photographer/videographer, printed materials, site visit catering
- **KPIs:** 2 case studies published; 1-2 site visits with 15+ attendees total; 5+ qualified leads from site visits; 300+ LinkedIn followers; 50+ newsletter subscribers; 3+ inbound B2B quote requests

### Phase 3: "Scale the Pipeline" (September – December 2026)
- **Objective:** Convert awareness into pipeline and close the year at 2x target
- **Audience:** Both personas, emphasis on Sofie (investors) for Q4 tax-year planning
- **Key Message:** "We handle the complexity, you focus on the investment"
- **Channels:** All channels at full cadence
- **Timeline:** September 1 – December 31
- **Tactics:**
  1. Attend Realty (September) — network, distribute case studies, connect on LinkedIn post-event
  2. Publish investor-specific content on ROI and new-build investment
  3. Create downloadable "Developer Partnership Guide" (gated PDF for lead capture)
  4. LinkedIn Ads pilot: target developers/investors in region (€500-1000/month test)
  5. Continue monthly newsletter + 2 blog posts/month
  6. Nurture all leads from Phase 2 with personalized follow-ups
  7. Plan Futurebuild Belgium presence (January 2027)
  8. Q4 review: assess pipeline, refine strategy for 2027
- **Budget:** Realty attendance, LinkedIn Ads (€500-1000/month), Developer Partnership Guide design, Futurebuild planning
- **KPIs:** 10+ qualified leads from Realty + LinkedIn Ads; 500+ LinkedIn followers; 150+ newsletter subscribers; 10+ inbound quote requests total; pipeline at 2x baseline by Dec 31
